Welcome to LeafLore!

LeafLore is your premium personal reading sanctuary. Let's take a quick tour of your new dashboard features to help you grow your reading habits.

The Focus Oasis

Drown out the noise with the Focus timer. Choose a book, set a Pomodoro, and mix ambient soundscapes (rain, wind, and campfire) synthesized directly in your browser.

Your Reading Garden

Grow your daily habits! The Reading Activity Heatmap visualizes your progress over the year. Click on any block to see detailed metrics and watch your foliage thrive.

AI & Analytics

LeafLore uses advanced predictive models to guess your completion rates, estimate finish dates, and recommend personalized next reads based on genres you complete.

Achievements & Themes

Unlocking achievements (e.g., logging reading sessions, maintaining streaks, completing books) unlocks rare seed theme packs (Meadow, Zen, Autumn, Library, Royal) to customize your Greenhouse Oasis canvas!

Offline Sync & Exports

LeafLore is an installable offline-first PWA. Your logs save to IndexedDB and sync automatically when online. Plus, you can download CSV, Markdown, or JSON backups anytime from the Settings page.
